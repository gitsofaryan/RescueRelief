How It Works

Our platform uses cutting-edge technologies to map the locations of rescue agencies in real-time during calamities. By staying up-to-date and informed, you can ensure you are always prepared for the unexpected.

Registered agencies provide critical information, such as their current location and resource availability, allowing users to make informed decisions in precarious situations. Collaboration is our advantage in these trying times.
